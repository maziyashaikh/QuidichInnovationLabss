Quidich Tracker

Key Features

01

AI-powered Player Tracking

Dynamically tracks and displays player positions during live games using ML and CV.

02

Advanced Visualisations

Utilises Unreal Engine for photorealistic and immersive visualisations of the stadium.

03

Sponsor Integration

Allows for branding and sponsor logos within visualisations to subsidise broadcast costs.

04

Data Integration

Offers cumulative narratives through dynamic visualisation of real-time insights as well as historical data.

05

Commentator Tool

Empowers commentators with real-time plot interaction, enhancing the narrative during live broadcasts.

06

Modular Design

Scalable to various sports beyond cricket, including baseball, football, and other track and field events.
